HISTORY
RYIAN'S BARBERSHOP (RBS) was founded on February 2021 and opened on January 24, 2022 in Poblacion, Boljoon, Cebu. It was built to meet the needs of the growing community. Ryian's Barbershop offers affordable products so that the community can access them.
LIFE OF THE FOUNDER
Mr. Renan Chen Faburada is the owner of Ryian’s Barbershop. He is a hardworking and responsible family man with two children in elementary school. His motivation is to provide for his family while serving the community through affordable, quality grooming services.
THE SUCCESS STORY OF THE BUSINESS
Ryian’s Barbershop started as a small neighborhood business and has grown into a trusted place for affordable grooming and basic household goods. Through Mr. Faburada’s hard work, the business has gained loyal customers and continues to support the local community.
CHALLENGES MET BY THE FOUNDER
Mr. Faburada faced competition, customers buying on credit, and balancing business with family life. Despite challenges, he manages the business with planning, dedication, and hard work.
